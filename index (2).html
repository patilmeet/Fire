About SnapShare

SnapShare allows you to upload and view photos.

Live Sync Enabled

This app is connected to a decentralized relay network.

Photos you upload are compressed and broadcast to other users currently online.

Note: As this uses a public demo network, photos may disappear after some time or if the network resets.
